Our Story

"The story begins with Sandeep, one of our co-founders, noticing a huge problem in India’s logistics space – SMEs were struggling to transport their product! To resolve this problem and provide logistics companies with the kind of support they needed to flourish, Sandeep joined with Vikas, our second-cofounder, to create Epictus Solutions.

And so, after trials, errors and lots of learning – Truck Suvidha was born. Today, it is the largest online transport marketplace community with over 10 Lakhs+ Indian transporters on it's platform. In the Indian logistics space, we aim to be more than just a product – we want to revolutionize the way B2B transportation happens in India!

With the Truck Suvidha app, users should no longer struggle to get or provide logistics services. Logistics is the backbone of the Indian economy, and we're here to strengthen it! By providing users with the technology for booking trucks and loads online, we're bridging the gap between lorry and load businesses, and helping them serve customers better."
